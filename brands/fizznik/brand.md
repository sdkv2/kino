# fizznik — brand guidelines

Space-age fruit soda, straight out of a 1962 rocket diner. Vintage warmth meets bold pop-art punch.

- Voice: giddy, retro, exclamatory. Short bouncy lines, ad-jingle energy. "Blast off!", "Pop the cap!", "Zing in every sip!". Playful, never corporate.
- Look: deep cosmic plum background, warm cream ink, and three saturated candy accents — cherry red (#FF4E6A), tangerine-gold (#FFB000), turquoise (#00B7A6). Chunky ultra-bold type (Anton). Everything bounces, pops, and overshoots — nothing sits still.
- Captions: word-by-word in a rounded highlight box, pop/wave entrances. Keep them 2-4 words, SHOUTY and fun. Emphasise the flavour words and the brand name.
- Avoid: muted/minimal restraint, thin type, health claims, anything that reads as "premium wellness". This is candy fun, loud and proud.
